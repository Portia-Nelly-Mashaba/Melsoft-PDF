About the Programme

The SNPL programme enables qualifying students to access funded education immediately, with structured repayment beginning only after securing employment. The goal is to remove financial barriers to entry for high-potential learners.

Key Benefits

  • Zero upfront tuition fees for accepted applicants
  • Affordable income-based repayments that only kick in once you are employed
  • Debt waived entirely if employment is not secured within 24 months of graduation
  • Access to mentorship, career coaching, and job placement support.
  • Flexible repayment pause available during qualifying life events

Eligibility Criteria

We keep things simple. To apply, you generally need to meet most of the following:

  1. Accepted into a partner institution
  2. Household income below the qualifying threshold (see table below)
  3. Enrolled in a programme on the approved courses list

Income Thresholds by Household Size

Household Size Maximum Annual Income Funding Tier Max Coverage
1 – 2 members R 120,000 Tier A 100%
3 – 4 members R 180,000 Tier B 80%
5+ members R 240,000 Tier C 60%
